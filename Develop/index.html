About me

Hi there! I'm Lina, a passionate and creative person exploring the world of technology, gaming, and coding. As a proud polyamorous transwoman and LGBTQIA+ advocate, I've been building a safe and welcoming space on Twitch where I stream as a variety gamer, dive into random coding adventures, and share my love for all things fun with my amazing community!

By day, I'm currently studying programming at Columbia University, working toward a futrure in IT and Full Stack Programming. My journey into technology began with my desire to combine my curiosity with practical problem-solving, leading me to discover the power and flexibility that programming has to offer.

When I'm not busy coding you can find me playing some cozy games on Twitch, like Stardew Valley, celebrating the little joys in life with my community.

I'm all about creating a safe space that is inclusive, fun, and packed with personality. Whether it's Gaming, coding, or sharing knowledge, my goal is to inspire others to embrace their true selves!
Now let's make something amazing together, one line of code or one stream at a time!
